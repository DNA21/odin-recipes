Honey Garlic Chicken

Description

This is a delicious chicken breast recipe where the chicken gets all it's flavor from the sauce that it is cooked in. Serve over rice or with your favorite vegetables for a tasty dinner everyone will enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (white vinegar is okay)
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 lb chicken breast (2 pieces)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Cut the breasts in half horizontally to create 4 steaks in total. Sprinkle each side with salt and pepper.
  2. Place flour in shallow dish. Coat chicken in flour and shake off excess.
  3. melt most of the butter in a large skillet over high heat - hold back about 1 teaspoon for later
  4. Place chicken in skillet and cook for 2 - 3 minutes until golden. Turn and cook the other side for 1 minute.
  5. Turn heat down to medium.
  6. Make a bit of room in the pan and add garlic and top with remaining dab of butter. Stir garlic briefly once butter melts.
  7. Add vinegar, soy sauce and honey. Stir/shake pan to combine. Bring sauce to simmer, then simmer for 1 minute or until slightly thickened.
  8. Turn chicken to coat in sauce. If the sauce gets too thick, add a touch of water and stir.
  9. Remove from the stove immediately.
